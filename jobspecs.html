Job Specifications

Web Developer

A web developer is a pro at building and maintaining websites and web apps. They use languages like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to create functional, user-friendly online experiences. They work with designers and stakeholders to ensure sites meet client needs and stay current with tech trends for innovative solutions.

IT Technician

An IT technician supports and maintains computer systems and networks, troubleshooting issues, installing software and hardware, and ensuring security. They also provide technical support to users and manage server setups and cybersecurity measures.
